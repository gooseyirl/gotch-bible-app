# Privacy Policy for Gotch Bible

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Gotch Bible ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information from users of Gotch Bible.**

The app operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track user behavior or analytics
- Require user accounts or registration
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Transmit any data to external servers
- Share any information with third parties

## Local Data Storage

The app stores workout history and exercise preferences locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by the app
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

## Permissions

Gotch Bible does not request any device permissions beyond what is necessary for basic functionality (screen display and audio playback).

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
