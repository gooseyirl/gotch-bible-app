## Long Description
*(4000 characters max)*

Gotch Bible brings Karl Gotch's legendary deck-of-cards conditioning method to your phone.

Shuffle a standard 52-card deck. Each suit maps to an exercise — Push-ups, Squats, Sit-ups, and Burpees by default. Flip through the deck and complete the reps shown on each card. Finish all 52 cards for a total of 364 reps and a complete workout.

**Features:**
- 3-second countdown before each workout
- Decisecond-precision timer
- Track reps per exercise across the full deck
- Save your top 10 fastest completion times
- Customise exercises for each suit to fit your training
- Review detailed breakdowns of past workouts
- Celebration animation on completion
- No ads, no tracking, completely free

**Customise your workout:**
Tap Settings to assign any exercise to each suit. Replace Burpees with Deadlifts, Squats with Lunges — whatever fits your training style.

**Privacy:**
No data leaves your device. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.
